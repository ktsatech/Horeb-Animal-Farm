Frequently Asked Questions

Read quick, authoritative answers compiled by Pr. Peter Musoke on ordering, transport, and farm consults.

Can I get guide on how to build farm structures?

Yes, absolutely! We can give you a perfect guide basing on the required resources, available resources and the standard required for a particular animal

Are Flemish Giant Rabbits easy to maintain in Uganda's climate?

Flemish Giants thrive wonderfully when raised in well-ventilated, spacious timber cages sheltered from direct equatorial rain and sun. Our consultation package includes full framing designs and immunization calendars to prevent coccidiosis and other local rabbit ailments.

How do we purchase organic compost manure in wholesale volumes?

Wholesale compost manure requests can be pre-ordered directly via our contact form. We pack in robust mesh sacks or coordinate small transport trucks (canter/fuso) for direct deliveries to coffee, banana, or tea fields surrounding Bushenyi and Sheema districts.
