Our Milestones

Our Journey of Excellence

Tracing back 18 years of growth, community outreach, and agricultural innovations in Bushenyi.

2008

Humble Farm Foundation

Horeb Animal Farm is established with a small flock of 100 layer poultry birds in Nyakabirizi, Bushenyi, providing fresh eggs to local schools.

2015

Poultry Expansion

Expanded chicken cages, upgraded feeding infrastructure, and increased layer bird capacity significantly to meet local organic demand.

2018

Pig Unit Launch

Introduced Large White pigs to expand biological diversity, swine genetics, and natural compost soil fertilization.

2019

Strategic Pandemic Focus

Shifted focus on local feed formulations, optimized breeding protocols, and clinical veterinary study on-site during lockdowns.

2021

Full Section Restoration

Re-activated all sections, upgraded bio-security protocols, and established study visits for graduate youth and schools.

2025

Flemish Giant Rabbits

Introduced world-class Flemish Giant rabbits to revolutionize high-yielding meat and companion rabbit breeding in Bushenyi.

2026

Commercial Rabbit Hub

Expanding custom multi-tier cage installations and bulk breeding facilities to satisfy regional agribusiness demand.

Our Mission

To produce and propagate superior livestock genetics, premium agricultural composting solutions, and exceptional farm training to uplift smallholders and commercial farm operators alike across East Africa.

Our Vision

To be Western Uganda's ultimate model farm of bio-safe farming protocols, circular farming waste recycling, and clinical veterinary breeding expertise.

Core Values

  • Genetic Quality: Strict lineage tracing and pedigree tracking.
  • Biosecurity First: Safeguarding flock and herd health profiles.
  • Environmental Integrity: Circular agricultural waste recycle loops.
  • Advisory Outreach: Dedicated community farmer training.
